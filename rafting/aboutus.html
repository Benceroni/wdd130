About Us
Born in the heart of idaho
Dogwater rafting was born in boise idaho in september of 2021. We've never actually guided a white water rafting trip but we're pretty confident it'll go well when someone actually pays us to go.
Founded with Doggos in mind
Dog Water rafting is and always has been about the dogs. Dog Water Rafting proudly donates a strong percentage of our profits to benefit dogs in shelters to put more dogs like scout in loving arms and families.
Meet our Founders
Micah "The Madlad River Rafter" Jenks- Founder
Alternatively Going by hickory ham mike, Micah "the madlad river rafter" Jenks has shredded some gnar in his day. He's the proud papa to scout, the legendary dog represented in Dog Water Rafting's logo. The madlad river rafter grew up in Idaho and traveled around the country seeking out the wildest white water. His most adventerous trip to date included being dropped with his kayak by helicopter into a remote river in alaska.
Steve "The Big Steeze" Bolander- Cofounder
